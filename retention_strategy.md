# Retention Strategy

## Segment 1: Champions

### Characteristics

* Recent purchases
* High frequency
* High spending
* Strong engagement

### Retention Action

* VIP rewards
* Early access launches
* Loyalty recognition

### Expected Value

Highest revenue retention.

---

## Segment 2: Loyal Customers

### Characteristics

* Frequent purchases
* Moderate-to-high spending
* Stable engagement

### Retention Action

* Membership upgrades
* Cross-selling campaigns

### Expected Value

Strong long-term revenue growth.

---

## Segment 3: New Customers

### Characteristics

* Recently acquired
* Limited purchase history

### Retention Action

* Onboarding campaigns
* Product education

### Expected Value

Increase second purchase probability.

---

## Segment 4: At-Risk Customers

### Characteristics

* Previously active
* Increasing inactivity

### Retention Action

* Personalized offers
* Reminder campaigns

### Expected Value

Prevent churn before disengagement.

---

## Segment 5: Dormant Customers

### Characteristics

* Long inactivity period
* Low engagement

### Retention Action

* Win-back campaigns
* Limited budget allocation

### Expected Value

Selective recovery opportunities.

---

## Segment 6: High-Value Unhappy Customers

### Characteristics

* High spend
* High support complaints
* Elevated refund activity

### Retention Action

* Customer Success intervention
* Priority support

### Expected Value

Protect valuable revenue relationships.

---

# Campaign Budget Prioritization

Assume Retention Budget = $10,000

| Segment            | Budget Allocation |
| ------------------ | ----------------- |
| High-Value Unhappy | 35%               |
| At-Risk            | 25%               |
| Loyal              | 15%               |
| Champions          | 10%               |
| New Customers      | 10%               |
| Dormant            | 5%                |

## Priority Order

1. High-Value Unhappy
2. At-Risk
3. Loyal
4. Champions
5. New Customers
6. Dormant

This prioritization maximizes retained revenue while minimizing churn risk.
